    Removes the signal chain signified by the input proc pair from its predecessors and successors.

    Removes the signal chain signified by the input proc pair from its predecessors and successors. It does so by removing the sources of the _1 scan named "in" and the sinks of the _2 scan named "out". It re-connects the predecessors and successors thus found.
